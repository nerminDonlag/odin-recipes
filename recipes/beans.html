Beans Recipe

Description

This is a description of how to make beans.

Ingredients

  • raw beans
  • onions
  • garlic
  • carrots

Steps

  1. dice the onions and carrots
  2. peal the garlic
  3. soak the beans
  4. fry the onions, carrots and garlic
  5. boil everything in a pot
  6. enjoy
