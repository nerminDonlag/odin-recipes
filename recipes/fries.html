Fries Recipe

Description

This is a description of how to make french fries.

Ingredients

  • potatos
  • salt
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. peal and wash the potatos
  2. slice potatos into strips
  3. heat the oil in a deep pot
  4. fry the potato strips
  5. put salt on the fried potatos
  6. enjoy
